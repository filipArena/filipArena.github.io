Vehicle tracking in traffic videos
This project involves tracking and counting vehicles through time using a top-down video stream of an intersection with no occlusions. In those conditions, it achieves almost perfect results with real-time performance.
  • Demo: Counting car video on Twitter
  • Technologies: Shapely for geometry math, Re3 for tracking and YOLOv3 for detection.
